ABOUT US

At the School of Buddhism, established in 2012, we provide a nurturing environment where students of all ages can explore Buddhist teachings, mindfulness practices, and ethical living. Whether you're new to Buddhism or seeking to deepen your understanding, our Sunday School offers a welcoming space for personal growth and reflection.

OUR MISSION

To inspire and educate individuals through the timeless teachings of the Buddha, fostering wisdom, compassion, and mindfulness in daily life.
